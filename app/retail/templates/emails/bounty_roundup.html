The Gitcoin Team has made another hire for the core team with the addition of Alisa March as a Product Design Lead. Alisa has done a few design bounties already, and we are elated to have her on the Gitcoin Team. Welcome, Alisa!

This week, we've reorganized the bounty flow to make it more clear whats being worked on and what's not.

  1. Open: Bounty is funded, and available for a new developer to come tackle.
  2. Work Started Bounty is funded, Coding has been started by at least one developer.
  3. Work Submitted: Bounty is funded, Code has been completed, and bounty hunters have *submitted* a claim
  4. Work Done: The funder has accepted the work submission. The staked funds have been paid out.

With the addition of Alisa to team, we are looking to tighten up the usability across the community. If you've got feedback, we'd love to hear it.
